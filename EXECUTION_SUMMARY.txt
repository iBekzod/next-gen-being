================================================================================
CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS
================================================================================

1. PUBLISHING FREQUENCY IS EVERYTHING
   - 2-3 posts/week = Growth in 2-3 months
   - 1 post/week = Growth in 4-6 months
   - <1 post/week = Growth in 6-12 months
   - Commit to 2-3/week for 3 months minimum

2. CONSISTENCY BEATS PERFECTION
   - 2 good posts > 1 perfect post
   - Quantity at quality level > rare perfection
   - Regular updates signal active site to Google
   - Consistency builds audience trust

3. QUALITY MATTERS WITHIN CONSISTENCY
   - Don't publish garbage just for frequency
   - Aim for solid, helpful, thorough posts
   - Show expertise and original insights
   - Cite credible sources, be transparent

4. FOLLOW THE FORMULA
   - Use title formulas from templates
   - Use meta description templates
   - Follow 5-step optimization process
   - Add internal links consistently
   - Don't skip steps to save time

5. NICHE FOCUS WORKS BETTER
   - Don't try to beat TechCrunch
   - Focus on specific angles (AI, startups, dev tools)
   - Build authority in your niche
   - Expand from positions of strength
