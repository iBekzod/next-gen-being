## Content Type Distribution

**For your 24-36 posts over 12 weeks, aim for:**

- **How-To Posts:** 40% (10-14 posts)
  - "How to [do something]"
  - Teaching format, actionable steps
  - Target: High intent keywords

- **List Posts:** 25% (6-9 posts)
  - "7 [nouns] every [audience] needs"
  - Roundup format, comparative data
  - Target: Moderate intent keywords

- **Comparison Posts:** 15% (4-5 posts)
  - "[Tool A] vs [Tool B]"
  - Detailed comparison tables
  - Target: Comparison keywords

- **News/Trend Posts:** 15% (4-5 posts)
  - "[Company] released/announced [news]"
  - Timely, breaking news
  - Target: News keywords, brand searches

- **Guide/Pillar Posts:** 5% (1-2 posts)
  - "The Complete Guide to [Topic]"
  - 2,000-2,500 words, comprehensive
  - Target: Main pillar keywords

## Tips for Success

1. **Batch writing:** Set aside 2-3 hours to write 2 posts at once
2. **Templates:** Use the title formulas and meta templates consistently
3. **Scheduling:** Use Filament to pre-schedule posts
4. **Team alignment:** Share this calendar with all writers
5. **Flexibility:** Adjust plan based on trending topics, reader feedback
6. **Quality first:** 2 great posts > 5 mediocre posts
7. **Consistency:** Publish on same days/times when possible
8. **Promotion:** Share every post on your channels
